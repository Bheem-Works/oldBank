-------------BUDGET--------------
=> THE WORD OF BUDGET IS DERIVED FROM THE FRENCH WORD THE BIGOT WHICH MEANS A LEATHER BAG. 
        AND THE BUDGET MEANS THE FINANCIAL STATEMENT WHICH CONTAINS THE ESTIMATIONS INCOMES OR EXPENDITURE OF THE GOVERNMENT FOR THE
        COMING FISCAL YEAR. 
        
----------TYPES OF BUDGET------
        (I) SURPLUS BUDGET 
                => INCOME IS GREATER THEN THE EXPENDITURE 
        (II) DEFICIT BUDGET 
                => INCOME IS LESS THEN THE EXPENDITURE WHICH MEANS (EXPENDITURE > INCOME)
        (III) BALANCE BUDGET 
                => INCOME IS EQUAL TO THE EXPENDITURE (INCOME == EXPENDITURE)


----------------COMPONENTS PART OF BUDGET----------------
        => THE main components of a budget are Revenue Budget and Capital Budget, which include revenue receipts,expenditure,capital   receipts,          and capital expenditure.
        
-------------PROCESS OF BUDGET FORMULATION IN NEPAL---------------
        => IN NEPAL FISCAL YEAR COUNT FROM THE FIRST SHARAWAN TO THE ENDS OF THE ASHAR.
        
                THE PROCESS OF BUDGET FORMULATION IS DISCUSSED AS BELOW:-
                 (I) ESTIMATIONS OF OVER ALL INCOME AND EXPENDITURE 
                 (II) PRIORITY ESTABLISHMENT 
                 (III) PREPARATION OF PROJECTS
                 (IV) SELECTIONS OF PROJECTS 
                 (V) PREPARATION OF FINAL BUDGET
                 (VI) AUTHORIZATION AND IMPLEMENTATION
                 
------------------MEANING OF FOREIGN TRADE----------------
        => THE TRANSACTIONS OF THE GOODS AND SERVICES HAPPEN BETWEEN ACROSS THE BOUNDARY OF A FAMILY OR A COUNTRY 
                IS CALLED A FOREIGN TRADE. 
                
-------------IMPORTANCE OF FOREIGN TRADE------------
        => IMPORTANCE OF FOREIGN TRADE : 
                (I) EXPANSION ON MARKET 
                (II) BENEFICIAL TO CONSUMER 
                (III) GAIN IN TECHNOLOGY 
                (IV) FREE CHOICE 
                (V) EMPLOYMENT GENERATOR 
                (VI) SUPPLY OF NECESSITY GOODS 
                (VII) IMPROVEMENT IN LIVING STANDARD. 
                
---------------BOT(BALANCE OF TRADE)-------------------
        => IT IS A DIFFERENCE BETWEEN OF THE VALUE OF EXPORTS AND THE IMPORTS OF A GOODS ONLY IN A COUNTRY. 

----------------------FEATURES---------------------
        => (I). INCLUDES ONLY GOODS (NO SERVICE)
           (II). PART OF BOP
           (III). FAVORABLE AND UNFAVORABLE 
           (IV). EASY TO CALCULATE 
           
--------------TYPES OF DUTY---------
        => (I). FAVORABLE BOT : 
                        -> EXPORTS MORE AND IMPORT LESS(GOOD) 
           (II). UNFAVORABLE BOT : 
                        -> IMPORT MORE AND EXPORT LESS(BAD)
           (III). BALANCE BOT : 
                        -> IMPORT AND EXPORT ARE BALANCE (NO GOOD NO BAD IT IS JUST EQUAL) 
                        
-----------------BOP(BALANCE OF PAYMENT)-------------------
        => RECORD ALL THE ECONOMICS TRANSACTION HAPPEN BETWEEN A COUNTRY AND THE REST OF THE OTHER'S WORLD.
        
--------------------FEATURES----------------- 
        => (I). WIDER CONCEPT 
           (II). INCLUDES THE VISIBLE + INVISIBLE ITEMS 
           (III). SYSTEMATIC RECORDS 
           (IV). ALWAYS BALANCE 
           
-------------TYPES--------------------
        => (I). CURRENT ACCOUNT => IT INCLUDES THE VISIBLE AND NON-VISIBLE  AND REMITTANCE.
          (II). CAPITAL ACCOUNT => IT INCLUDES THE CAPITAL INVESTMENT THROUGH THE STOCKS, MARKET,LOAN,SHARES,BONDS ETC...
          (III). CASH ACCOUNT   => IT INCLUDES THE STOCK OF FOREIGN EXCHANGE RATE RECEIVES LIKE GOLD, SILVER, F.D.I ETC....
-------------- TYPES OF THE BOP--------------
        => (I) FAVORABLE => RECEIPT IS MORE THAN THE PAYMENT
           (II) UNFAVORABLE => PAYMENT IS MORE THEN THE RECEIPT 
           (III) BALANCE => P === R 
           
------------------DIFFERENCE BETWEEN BOP AND BOT-------------
        | Basis             | BOT                 | BOP              |

-----------------GOVERNMENT BORROWING---------------

                SO FULL FILL THE DEFICIT THE COUNTRY WILL TAKE A LOAN OR BORROW MONEY FROM INSIDE OR ANOTHER COUNTRY 
                AND THAT IS CALLED A GOVERNMENT BORROWING.

        1. INTERNAL := MARKET BORROWING -> MONEY FROM INSTITUTIONS AND SELLS THE BONDS AND THE FUNDS
