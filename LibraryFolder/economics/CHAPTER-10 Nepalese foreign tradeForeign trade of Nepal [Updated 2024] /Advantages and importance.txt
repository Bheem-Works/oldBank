
Advantages and importance of foreign employment
1. Reduction of unemployment in country. 
2. Gain in skills. 
3. Poverty reductions 
4. Improving in the living standards.
5. Development in agriculture
6. Increase in remittance

Disadvantages of foreign employment 
1. Shortage of man power inside the country 
2. Brain drain 
3. Disintegration of families 
4. Suffering and difficulties to children. 
5. Deaths and difficulties 
6. Cheating and abuses

=> Planning is a set of activities in order to full the objective in the given firm using the available resources.
