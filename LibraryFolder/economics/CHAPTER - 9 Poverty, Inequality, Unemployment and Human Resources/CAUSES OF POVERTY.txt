        CAUSES OF POVERTY
        
        
        Causes of poverty : 
        1. Lack of employment
        2. Lack of skills and training
        3. lack of agriculture production
        4. Lack of opportunity
        5. Slow peace of industrialization
        6. Climate changes
        7. Unequal distributions of resources
        8. Unnecessary consumption's
        
        # Unemployment:
                => It is a situations where people are willing to work at the existing rate but can not get any job. 
                        "It is also called a voluntary/open (unemployment)"
                
                There are various Types of Unemployment:
                1. Distinguished Unemployment
                2. Seasonal unemployment
                3. Frictional unemployment
                4. Cyclical unemployment
                5. Structural unemployment
                6. Voluntary unemployment
                7. In-Voluntary unemployment
                8. Open unemployment
                
        # Causes of unemployment:
                1. High growth rate of populations
                2. Lack of educations and skills
                3. Lack of pace of industrial development
                4. Subsistence Agriculture
                5. Small size of land holding
                6. Lack of Agriculture credit
                7. Lack of corruptions(nepotism)
                8. Lack of opportunity 
                9. Lack of credit and resources
                
        # Measures for Poverty Reduction
                1. High economic growth
                2. Development of agriculture sectors
                3. Create Employment opportunity
                4. Targeted programs for the poor people
                5. Social security
                6. Equal distribution of national income
                7. Investment in human development
