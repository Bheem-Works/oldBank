CONCEPT OF POVERTY


Poverty ?
        => Poverty is a situation where people are not able to full fill their basics needs of life as :- foods, shelter , clothing etc.
           "According to the world bank it is also vulnerability,lack of power,voice and representation"
           
           There are two types of poverty: 
           A. Absolute poverty : 
                => It is a situations where people can not be able to full fill their basics needs and live under the poverty line. 
                
                --- Poverty line --- => It is the minimum income which is just sufficient to maintain their basics needs. 
          
          B. Relative poverty : 
                => It is a situation where people can meet their basics needs but they are being poor compared to others people in the same society.
