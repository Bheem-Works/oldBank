# Human Resources 

    => The actual and skill full people between the age of 15 to 59 years who can contribute to development are called the human resources. 
        The man power of a country who are active and skills full and those who can works and contribute to the economy is also known as human resources. 
        
        Role/Importance of natural resources :

        3. To develop industries.
        4. Development in techonology. 
        5. Development of agriculture sectors. 
        6. To make country self - dependent.
        7. Social reforms
